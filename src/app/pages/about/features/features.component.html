Membership Management Features

All the right tools to manage your membership activities

Member Profiles

Collect, update and manage profile information about your members including photos, family and location information.

Online Applications

Grow your community through online applications. Members with platform accounts can join with a single click.

Groups & Associations

Categorize all your members with custom groups and associations within your organisation.

SMS Messaging

Send Bulk SMS messages to your contact with customizable Sender IDs and dynamic broadcast lists.

Online Payments

Connect your payment platforms and start receiving payments instantly from your members all around the world.

Manage Finances

Track revenue, pledges and donations, generate reports and send SMS reminders & receipts to members instantly.
